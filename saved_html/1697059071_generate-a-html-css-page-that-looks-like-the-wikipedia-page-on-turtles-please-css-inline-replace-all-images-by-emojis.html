Turtles

🐢Turtles are reptiles of the order Testudines characterized by a special bony or cartilaginous shell developed from their ribs that acts as a shield.

🌍Geographic Distribution

Turtles are found in various parts of the world, including North America, South America, Europe, Asia, and Africa.

🍃Habitat

Turtles inhabit diverse ecosystems such as forests, wetlands, grasslands, and deserts.

🥬Diet

Turtles have a diverse diet that includes plant matter, insects, worms, fish, and small mammals.

🥚Reproduction

Turtles lay eggs on land, burying them in nests and leaving them to incubate until the hatchlings emerge.

🌡️Temperature Regulation

Turtles are ectothermic animals, meaning they rely on external sources of heat to regulate their body temperature.
