Turtles

Physical Description

Turtles are reptiles of the order Testudines characterized by a special bony or cartilaginous shell developed from their ribs that acts as a shield.

Species

There are over 360 known species of turtles, including sea turtles, freshwater turtles, and tortoises.

Habitat

Turtles can be found in various habitats such as oceans, ponds, rivers, and forests.

Conservation

Many species of turtles are endangered due to habitat destruction, pollution, and illegal trade.

Trivia

Turtles are known for their slow movement and long lifespans, with some species living over 100 years.
