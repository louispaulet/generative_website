Rabbits

Description

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae of the order Lagomorpha. They are well-known for their long ears 🐰. They typically have soft and fluffy fur 🌟.

Habitat

Rabbits can be found in various habitats including forests 🌲, meadows 🌿, and grasslands 🌾.

Behavior

Rabbits are known for their ability to hop 🐇. They are social animals and often live in groups called colonies 🏠. They are herbivores, primarily feeding on plants 🌱.

Reproduction

Rabbits reproduce quickly 🐣. They are capable of having multiple litters in a year, with each litter consisting of several kits 🐰.

Conservation Status

The conservation status of rabbits varies depending on the species. Some species are listed as endangered 🚫, while others are considered of least concern ✅.
