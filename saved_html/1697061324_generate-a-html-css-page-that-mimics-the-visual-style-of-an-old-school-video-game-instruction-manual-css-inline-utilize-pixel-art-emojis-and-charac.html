Game Instruction Manual

Controls

  • ⬆️ Move Up
  • ⬇️ Move Down
  • ⬅️ Move Left
  • ➡️ Move Right
  • 🅾️ Action Button
  • ❎ Cancel Button

Gameplay

Welcome to the exciting world of Pixel Adventure! In this game, you play as a brave hero fighting against evil monsters. Your mission is to save the princess and restore peace to the kingdom.

Explore different levels, defeat enemies, and collect power-ups to increase your strength. Be careful, as the monsters get tougher as you progress through the game.

Use your navigation buttons to move around and the action button to attack. Don't forget to visit the item shop to replenish your health and purchase useful items.

Tips

  1. Always keep an eye on your health bar.
  2. Collect coins to earn extra points.
  3. Use special power-ups wisely to overcome difficult challenges.
  4. Save your progress at the game's save points.
